********************** HOW TO PLAY **********************

The goal of this game is to kill as many enemies as possible.
Doing so will raise your score; you 
should attempt high scores.

Every 5 waves spawns a wave of enemies with double HP.
Every 10 waves spawns a wave of enemies with double damage, 
high speed, and decreased health.
Every 10 waves also spawns one more 
enemy.

You have 100HP at the start. Every wave, you heal for 10HP.

Controls:

Move up: w
 
Move down: s

Move right: d

Move left: a

Add fire element: 1

Add water element: 2

Add earth element: 3

Clear elements: q

Fire: LMB
